Our Core Values

The accepted principles or standards

0 - Organization

We the Management, Parents, Teachers and students must work as a team identified by a shared vision and mission.

D - Diligence

We the management, parents, teachers and students must be persistent and hardworking towards achieving our shared vision and mission

R - Responsibility

We the management, parents, teachers and students must be accountable and responsible to each other and our shared vision and mission

E - Excellence

We the management, parents, teachers and students must show that we are outstanding and superior to all by achieving our shared vision and mission.

R- Righteousness

We the management, parents, teachers and students must strictly observe morals according to the Christian and biblical standards in achieving our shared vision and mission.
